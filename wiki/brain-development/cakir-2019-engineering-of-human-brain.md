## Summary
Engineering ETV2-expressing vascular progenitors into human cortical organoids generates a vascular-like system with blood-brain barrier features, improving cell survival and maturation. Addresses the critical limitation of avascular organoids dying in inner regions.

## Key Contributions
- ETV2 co-differentiation → vascular-like network in hCOs
- BBB gene expression; reduced apoptosis in inner regions
- Improved neuronal maturation and organoid longevity

## Methods & Architecture
iPSC co-differentiation (neural + ETV2 vascular); 3D co-culture; immunostaining + scRNA-seq + BBB assays.

## Results
- Vascular-like network formed; BBB features present
- Cell survival extended vs non-vascularized organoids

## Limitations
Not true brain endothelium; incomplete BBB; no pericytes.
