## Summary
snRNA-seq atlas of human temporal lobe with 75 cell subtypes across pediatric and adult stages, validated with spatial transcriptomics. Includes African ancestry samples. LTK and FREM excitatory neuron subtypes show elevated cognition/synaptic plasticity genes in pediatric tissue, revealing cell-type-specific maturation dynamics.

## Key Contributions
- 75-subtype joint pediatric-adult temporal cortex atlas with spatial transcriptomics validation
- African ancestry representation (improves inclusivity of brain cell maps)
- LTK+ and FREM+ excitatory neurons: elevated cognition/synaptic plasticity genes in pediatric tissue
- Cell-type-specific maturation dynamics invisible to bulk transcriptomics

## Methods & Architecture
snRNA-seq on temporal lobe (diverse ancestry); 75 subtypes by clustering; spatial transcriptomics validation; pediatric vs adult DE analysis.

## Results
- LTK, FREM subtypes are maturation-relevant excitatory neuron populations
- Gene expression dynamics during maturation differ per cell type

## Limitations
Temporal lobe only; cross-sectional; rare cell types undersampled.
