## Summary
Whole adult mouse brain cell atlas using MERFISH (>1,100 genes, ~10M cells) integrated with scRNA-seq. Defines 5,000+ cell clusters, 300+ major cell types with full spatial resolution registered to Allen CCF. Identifies spatial modules, gradients, and cell-type-specific ligand-receptor interactions.

## Key Contributions
- Whole mouse brain spatial atlas: 5,000+ clusters, 300+ cell types, ~10M cells
- MERFISH + scRNA-seq integration for transcriptome-wide spatial resolution
- CCF registration: systematic cell-type composition per brain region
- Spatial modules and gradients across brain-wide territories
- Cell-type-specific ligand-receptor interaction network

## Methods & Architecture
MERFISH (>1,100 gene panels); Allen scRNA-seq reference integration; CCF registration; spatial module/gradient analysis; ligand-receptor inference.

## Results
- First comprehensive spatial cell atlas of whole mouse brain
- Spatial modules with distinct cell-type compositions
- Interaction network across 100s of cell-type pairs

## Limitations
Mouse only; 1,100-gene panel; interactions not functionally validated.
