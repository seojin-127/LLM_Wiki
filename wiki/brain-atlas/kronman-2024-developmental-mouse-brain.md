## Summary
DevCCF: 3D developmental common coordinate framework for mouse brain from E11.5 to P56, built from MRI + light-sheet microscopy templates with 3D anatomical segmentations. Maps Allen CCFv3 and spatial transcriptome data to P56 atlas. Open-access web visualizer.

## Key Contributions
- 3D atlas templates at E11.5, E13.5, E15.5, E18.5, P4, P14, P56
- MRI + light-sheet microscopy; morphologically averaged undistorted templates
- 3D anatomical segmentations; GABAergic neuron emergence mapped
- Allen CCFv3 + spatial transcriptome data integrated into P56 stereotaxic framework

## Methods & Architecture
- Multi-animal MRI averaging + high-resolution light-sheet microscopy per age
- Cross-stage 3D registration; web visualizer for open access

## Results
- First developmental 3D reference framework (E11.5–P56)
- GABAergic neuron emergence trajectories across embryonic stages
- Allen CCFv3 and spatial data mapped to stereotaxic P56 atlas
