## Summary
Review of 3D brain organoid technology and applications. Covers vascularized/mixed-lineage organoids, synthetic biomaterials, microfluidics. Applications span neurodevelopmental disorders, viral infections, neurodegeneration. Discusses translational value and challenges.

## Key Contributions
- Technology overview: vascularized organoids, microfluidics, biomaterials
- Applications: preterm birth, viral neuroinflammation, neurodevelopment, neurodegeneration
