## Summary
Review of cerebral organoid technology advances for neurological disease modeling. Covers vascularization, microfluidics, mixed lineage models; applications in neurodevelopmental disorders, viral infections, neurodegeneration. Discusses translational value and limitations.

## Key Contributions
- Comprehensive overview of organoid models for neurological disease
- Discusses vascularized and mixed-lineage organoids
- Applications: neurodevelopment, viral neurotropism, neurodegeneration
