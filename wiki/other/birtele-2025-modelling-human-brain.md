## Summary
Comprehensive roadmap review of brain organoid technology (Nat. Rev. MCB 2025). Covers organoid biology for neocortex development, comparison with other model systems, bioengineering improvements (vascularization, assembloids, ALI-COs), high-throughput omics + CRISPR screening applications, and ethical considerations.

## Key Contributions
- Systematic review of brain organoid strengths/weaknesses vs. 2D, mouse, NHP
- Comprehensive coverage of bioengineering enhancements
- High-throughput applications: organoid + scRNA-seq + CRISPR screens
- Future directions + ethics of advanced organoids

## Methods & Architecture
- Roadmap review; state-of-art as of late 2024

## Results
- Organoids: best for 3D human context; limited by vasculature, circuit maturity, batch variability
- Key technologies: assembloids, ALI-COs, in vivo transplantation, multiomics screening

## Limitations
- Review article; no original data
