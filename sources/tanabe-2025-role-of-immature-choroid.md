## One-line Summary
Choroid plexus (ChP)-specific CAMDI knockout mice exhibit immature ChP development and ASD-like behaviors including delayed social critical period; immature ChP is recapitulated in ASD patient iPSC-derived organoids, and early postnatal metformin treatment improves both ChP maturation and behavior.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Cell Reports
- Published: January 28, 2025

## 2. Key Contributions
- Establishes choroid plexus immaturity as a pathological feature of ASD using ChP-specific CAMDI-KO mice
- First demonstration that immature ChP → delayed social critical period
- ASD patient iPSC-derived organoids recapitulate immature ChP phenotype (human validation)
- Early postnatal metformin treatment rescues ChP maturation and ASD-like behaviors

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- ChP-specific CAMDI knockout mouse model (conditional KO using ChP-Cre)
- Behavioral assays: social interaction, ultrasonic vocalizations, repetitive behaviors
- Critical period assessment: social behavior maturation timeline
- Human iPSC-derived choroid plexus organoids from ASD patients vs. controls
- Drug intervention: metformin (postnatal day 0–21)

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- CAMDI-KO mice: immature ChP, ASD-like behaviors (reduced social interaction, increased repetitive behaviors)
- CAMDI-KO mice show delayed social critical period (maturation shifted)
- ASD patient iPSC organoids show immature ChP gene expression signature (matching mouse phenotype)
- Metformin (P0–P21): improved ChP maturation markers and normalized social behavior without delaying critical period

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- CAMDI is one ASD risk gene; generalizability across ASD genetic heterogeneity unclear
- Organoid model lacks full ChP architecture and CSF secretion function
- Metformin mechanism of action on ChP maturation not fully elucidated

## 6. Related Work
- Choroid plexus organoids: Lancaster lab, Pasca lab
- ASD behavioral genetics: CHD8, DYRK1A models
- Critical period biology: Hensch lab

## 7. Glossary
- **ChP**: Choroid plexus — brain structure producing cerebrospinal fluid (CSF); matures before brain
- **CAMDI**: Centrosome-associated protein; ASD risk gene; regulates ChP development
- **Critical period**: Developmental window of heightened neural plasticity; social critical period in rodents ~P10–P30
- **Metformin**: AMPK activator; diabetes drug; shown to promote neural development in some models
- **CSF**: Cerebrospinal fluid — produced by ChP; provides nutrients and signaling molecules to developing brain
