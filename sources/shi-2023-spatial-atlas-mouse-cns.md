## One-line Summary
STARmap PLUS in situ sequencing (1,022 genes, 3D, 194 nm voxels) maps 1.09M cells across adult mouse brain and spinal cord; 230 molecular cell types and 106 molecular tissue regions; integrated with scRNA-seq atlas; viral tropism of AAV-PHP.eB delineated.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: October 2023

## 2. Key Contributions
- STARmap PLUS: in situ sequencing at 194 × 194 × 345 nm³ voxel size (subcellular resolution)
- 1,022 genes profiled in 3D across adult mouse brain and spinal cord
- 1.09M high-quality cells; 230 molecular cell types, 106 molecular tissue regions
- Systematic molecular spatial nomenclature of cell types and tissue architectures
- Integration with published scRNA-seq atlas (Allen Brain Atlas) via imputation of 11,844 genes
- AAV-PHP.eB viral tropism mapped at cell-type resolution

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- STARmap PLUS: click chemistry-based in situ sequencing in tissue sections; 3D reconstruction from serial sections
- ClusterMap for cell segmentation; 1,022-gene panel
- Integration: spatial measurements matched to scRNA-seq clusters for transcriptome-wide imputation

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 3D spatial atlas at subcellular voxel resolution across entire CNS
- 230 molecular cell types identified by single-cell gene expression
- 106 molecular tissue regions defined by spatial niche gene expression
- Novel tissue architectures identified not in established brain anatomy
- Transcriptome-wide imputation: 11,844 genes assigned spatial context
- AAV-PHP.eB tropism: preferential targeting of specific CNS cell types

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- 1,022 gene panel; transcriptome-wide coverage via imputation (not direct measurement)
- 3D reconstruction across serial sections: alignment errors possible
- Mouse only; human CNS spatial atlas lacking

## 6. Related Work
- Zhang et al. 2023 (MERFISH): whole mouse brain MERFISH atlas (complementary spatial)
- Yao et al. 2023: Allen Brain Cell Atlas (scRNA reference for integration)
- Zhou et al. 2023: brain-wide epigenomics + projections

## 7. Glossary
- **STARmap PLUS**: Spatially-resolved Transcript Amplicon Readout Mapping — in situ sequencing technology
- **ClusterMap**: Computational tool for single-cell segmentation in spatial sequencing data
- **AAV-PHP.eB**: Adeno-associated virus variant with CNS-wide tropism; used for gene delivery
- **Molecular tissue region**: Spatial domain defined by niche gene expression (vs. single-cell types)
