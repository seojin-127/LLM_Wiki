## One-line Summary
Allen Brain Cell Atlas: ~4M scRNA-seq cells + 4.3M MERFISH spatial cells from whole adult mouse brain; 34 classes, 338 subclasses, 1,201 supertypes, 5,322 clusters; combinatorial TF code defines cell types brain-wide.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: December 2023

## 2. Key Contributions
- Whole adult mouse brain cell-type atlas: ~7M cells profiled, ~4M passing QC (scRNA-seq) + 4.3M spatial (MERFISH)
- Hierarchical taxonomy: 34 classes, 338 subclasses, 1,201 supertypes, 5,322 clusters
- Dorsal-ventral brain dichotomy: dorsal has fewer but more divergent neuronal types; ventral has more but more related types
- Extraordinary neurotransmitter/neuropeptide co-expression diversity
- Combinatorial transcription factor code defines cell types across all brain regions
- Online platform: Allen Brain Cell Atlas (interactive visualization)

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- scRNA-seq (~7M cells total, ~4M QC-passing)
- MERFISH spatial transcriptomics: 4.3M cells across whole brain sections
- Hierarchical clustering: 4 levels (class → subclass → supertype → cluster)
- Integration of scRNA-seq and MERFISH for spatial type assignment

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 5,322 terminal clusters = highest-resolution whole-brain mouse cell type atlas
- High correspondence between transcriptomic identity and spatial specificity
- Dorsal brain: fewer, more divergent neuronal types; ventral: more numerous, more similar
- Neuropeptide/neurotransmitter co-expression: extraordinary diversity across types
- TF combinatorial code: key determinant of cell type identity brain-wide

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Adult mouse only; developmental and aging atlases separate efforts
- MERFISH: subset of genes measured; not whole transcriptome
- Non-human; human equivalent atlas under construction

## 6. Related Work
- Van Velthoven et al. 2025: telencephalic GABAergic atlas (subset, more detailed)
- Winter et al. 2023: SPN taxonomy (uses this atlas as reference)
- Zhang et al. 2023 (MERFISH, Nature): earlier mouse brain MERFISH atlas

## 7. Glossary
- **MERFISH**: Multiplexed Error-Robust FISH — spatial transcriptomics at single-cell resolution
- **Allen Brain Cell Atlas**: Online platform for interactive visualization of this dataset
- **Dorsal-ventral dichotomy**: Structural principle: dorsal brain (cortex/hippocampus) vs. ventral (subcortical) cell type organization
