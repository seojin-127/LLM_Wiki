## One-line Summary
Nature Perspective synthesizing recent advances in human and mammalian brain development, covering comprehensive cell atlases, novel developmental cell populations, conserved/divergent cross-species patterns, and linking these to neurodevelopmental and neuropsychiatric disorders.

## 1. Document Info

## 2. Key Contributions
- Perspective by major brain atlas/development consortium authors (Nowakowski, Bhaduri, Zeng, Kriegstein, Linnarsson, etc.)
- Synthesizes recent comprehensive cell atlases of developing human brain and model organisms (mouse, NHP)
- Highlights novel developmental cell populations and cross-species conservation/divergence
- Connects transcriptional control of cell fate to complex behaviors and NDD/psychiatric disorder risk
- Identifies conceptual/technical challenges and future directions: additional developmental stages, whole-brain, adolescence
