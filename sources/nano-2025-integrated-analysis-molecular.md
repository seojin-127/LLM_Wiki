## One-line Summary
Meta-analysis of 23 human cortical scRNA-seq datasets (7 developmental + 16 adult) generates 500+ gene co-expression meta-modules revealing dynamic cell subtype specification; meta-module 20 (FEZF2+/TSHZ3) drives deep layer neuron fate validated by cortical chimeroid experiments.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature Neuroscience
- Published: May 2025

## 2. Key Contributions
- Parallel meta-analysis of 7 developmental + 16 adult human cortical datasets → 500+ gene co-expression networks (meta-modules)
- Meta-modules show dynamic cell subtype specificity across cortical development (focused on peak neurogenesis)
- Meta-module 20: elevated in FEZF2+ deep layer neurons; includes TSHZ3 (neurodevelopmental disorder TF)
- Cortical chimeroid experiments validate that FEZF2 and TSHZ3 both required for meta-module 20 activity and deep layer specification (via distinct mechanisms)
- Demonstrates how meta-atlases enable mechanistic follow-up

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Meta-analysis: 23 published scRNA-seq datasets (7 developmental, 16 adult human cortex)
- Weighted gene co-expression network analysis (WGCNA-like) → 500+ meta-modules
- Temporal module dynamics: which modules are specific to development vs. adult
- Cortical chimeroid: human cells knocked out for FEZF2 or TSHZ3 transplanted into mouse cortex for in vivo validation
- Expression validated in primary human cortical tissues

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 500+ meta-modules; many show developmental stage-specific expression
- Meta-module 20: FEZF2+/TSHZ3+ deep layer neuron module; spatiotemporal expression pattern
- FEZF2 KO: disrupts deep layer specification (cell fate)
- TSHZ3 KO: disrupts module 20 activity via distinct mechanism (transcriptional regulation)
- Both genes required but via different modalities (cell fate vs. transcriptional control)

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Meta-analysis across datasets with different protocols; batch effects partially managed
- Chimeroid: mouse host; may not fully recapitulate human cortical environment
- Module validation limited to meta-module 20; others require further study

## 6. Related Work

## 7. Glossary
- **Meta-module**: Gene co-expression module identified across multiple independent datasets
- **FEZF2**: Transcription factor required for deep cortical layer (L5/L6) identity
- **TSHZ3**: Transcription factor associated with neurodevelopmental disorders; part of module 20
- **Chimeroid**: Chimeric organoid/organism: human KO cells in mouse cortex for in vivo validation
- **WGCNA**: Weighted gene co-expression network analysis
