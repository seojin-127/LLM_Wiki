## One-line Summary
CRISPRi screen of 44 TFs in human primary radial glia identifies ZNF219 as a key repressor of premature differentiation, with NR2E1 and ARX playing opposing roles in RG lineage plasticity; effector genes converge on neurodevelopmental disorder risk.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 2026

## 2. Key Contributions
- Pooled CRISPRi screen of 44 TFs expressed during human cortical neurogenesis
- ZNF219 identified as critical repressor of premature RG differentiation
- NR2E1 and ARX play opposing roles in RG lineage plasticity (self-renewal vs. differentiation)
- Convergent effector genes downstream of multiple TFs enriched for neurodevelopmental disorder risk variants
- GRN map linking TF perturbation to cortical cell fate outcomes

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Human primary radial glia culture system (not organoid)
- Pooled CRISPRi (dCas9-KRAB) lentiviral screen targeting 44 TFs
- Single-cell RNA-seq readout of perturbation effects (Perturb-seq style)
- Downstream effector gene identification and disease variant enrichment analysis

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- ZNF219 KD → premature neuronal differentiation, loss of RG identity
- NR2E1 KD → increased self-renewal; ARX KD → increased differentiation (opposing)
- Convergent effector genes across multiple TF KDs enriched for ASD/ID risk variants
- GRN topology maps TF→gene regulatory relationships in human cortical neurogenesis

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Primary RG culture system; in vivo validation needed
- 44 TFs screened; comprehensive GRN incomplete
- CRISPRi knockdown, not knockout; residual TF activity possible

## 6. Related Work
- Li et al. 2023 (CHOOSE): pooled CRISPR screen of ASD genes in cerebral organoids
- Jourdon et al. 2023: TF divergence in idiopathic ASD iPSC-derived neural cells
- Paulsen et al. 2022: ASD gene convergence in organoid development

## 7. Glossary
- **CRISPRi**: CRISPR interference — dCas9-KRAB fusion silences target gene without cutting DNA
- **RG (radial glia)**: Primary cortical neural stem cell; generates neurons and glia during development
- **ZNF219**: Zinc finger transcription factor; here identified as repressor of premature differentiation
- **Perturb-seq**: Pooled CRISPR screen with single-cell RNA-seq readout per perturbation
