## One-line Summary
MERFISH imaging of 1,100+ genes in ~10 million cells across whole adult mouse brain, integrated with scRNA-seq, generates a comprehensive cell atlas of 5,000+ transcriptionally distinct clusters in 300+ major cell types with full spatial resolution; enables cell-type interaction and ligand-receptor inference.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 14 December 2023 (Vol. 624)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Whole mouse brain cell atlas at molecular and spatial resolution: >5,000 cell clusters, >300 major cell types
- Used MERFISH (multiplexed error-robust FISH) to image >1,100 genes in ~10 million cells
- Registered atlas to Allen Mouse Brain Common Coordinate Framework (CCF)
- Identified spatial modules with distinct cell-type compositions and spatial gradients
- Inferred cell-type-specific ligand-receptor interactions across 100s of cell-type pairs

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- MERFISH: multiplexed error-robust fluorescence in situ hybridization on whole mouse brain sections
- Integration with Allen Institute scRNA-seq reference data
- CCF registration for systematic brain-region-level analysis
- Spatial module identification; ligand-receptor interaction inference

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 5,000+ transcriptionally distinct clusters; 300+ major cell types across whole brain
- Systematic cell-type composition quantification per brain region
- Spatial modules: distinct cell-type compositions in brain-wide territories
- Spatial gradients: gradual cell-type transitions across brain regions
- Cell-type-specific interaction network: 100s of cell-type pairs with predicted molecular basis

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Mouse atlas; human equivalent needed
- 1,100 genes panels miss some rare/novel markers
- Interaction inference is based on co-localization + ligand-receptor database, not functional validation

## 6. Related Work
- Allen Mouse Brain Atlas (in situ hybridization)
- Zeng/Tasic et al.: Allen scRNA-seq mouse brain atlas
- Zhang et al.: MERFISH technology development
- BICCN cross-species atlas

## 7. Glossary
- **MERFISH**: Multiplexed error-robust fluorescence in situ hybridization — spatial transcriptomics method
- **CCF**: Allen Mouse Brain Common Coordinate Framework — standardized 3D reference space
- **Spatial module**: Spatially contiguous territory with a distinct cell-type composition
- **Ligand-receptor inference**: Predicting cell-cell communication from co-expressed ligands/receptors
