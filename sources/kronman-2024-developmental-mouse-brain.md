## One-line Summary
DevCCF: 3D developmental common coordinate framework spanning E11.5 to P56 with MRI and light-sheet microscopy atlas templates; 3D anatomical segmentations; maps Allen CCFv3 and spatial transcriptome data to stereotaxic P56 atlas.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature Communications
- Published: 2024

## 2. Key Contributions
- DevCCF: 3D atlas templates for E11.5, E13.5, E15.5, E18.5, P4, P14, P56
- MRI + light-sheet fluorescence microscopy templates; morphologically averaged from multiple animals
- 3D anatomical segmentations downloadable + interactive web visualizer
- Use case: GABAergic neuron emergence mapped across embryonic stages
- Allen CCFv3 and spatial transcriptome cell-type data mapped to P56 atlas

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Multi-animal MRI averaging for undistorted 3D templates at each age
- Light-sheet microscopy for high-resolution fluorescence templates
- 3D registration and alignment across developmental stages

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- First 3D developmental reference framework spanning E11.5 to P56
- GABAergic neuron emergence mapped across embryonic stages
- Allen CCFv3 and spatial transcriptomics data integrated into P56 stereotaxic atlas
- Open-access web visualizer

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Template averaging may obscure individual variation
- Fewer animals at embryonic stages due to tissue availability

## 6. Related Work
- Yao et al. 2023: Allen Brain Cell Atlas (adult mouse CCFv3)
- Van Velthoven et al. 2025: telencephalic GABAergic atlas (uses adult framework)

## 7. Glossary
- **CCF**: Common Coordinate Framework — standardized 3D reference atlas for brain
- **DevCCF**: Developmental CCF — time-resolved 3D atlas across embryonic and postnatal stages
