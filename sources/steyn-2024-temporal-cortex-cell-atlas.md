## One-line Summary
snRNA-seq atlas of human temporal lobe (75 cell subtypes, including African ancestry samples) comparing pediatric and adult cortex; validated with spatial transcriptomics; identifies excitatory neuron subtypes (LTK, FREM) with elevated cognition/synaptic plasticity genes in pediatric tissue.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature Genetics
- Published: December 2024 (Vol. 56, 2718–2730)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Built joint pediatric-adult snRNA-seq atlas of 75 cell subtypes from human temporal lobe tissue
- Includes samples of African ancestry (improving diversity in brain cell maps)
- Validated cell types with spatial transcriptomics
- Identified LTK and FREM excitatory neuron subtypes with elevated cognition/synaptic plasticity genes in pediatric tissue
- Contributes to global efforts for inclusive human brain cell map

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- snRNA-seq on temporal lobe tissue (pediatric + adult donors, diverse ancestry)
- 75 cell subtypes identified via clustering
- Spatial transcriptomics validation
- Differential expression analysis: pediatric vs adult per cell type

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 75 cell subtypes defined across pediatric and adult temporal cortex
- LTK+ and FREM+ excitatory neuron subtypes show elevated cognition/synaptic plasticity genes in pediatric samples
- Global gene expression changes during brain maturation are cell-type-specific (masked in bulk analyses)
- Atlas includes African ancestry donors (previous atlases predominantly European)

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Temporal lobe only; other regions may differ
- Cross-sectional design (different donors for each time point)
- Sample sizes limited for rare cell types

## 6. Related Work
- Allen Brain Cell Atlas, BICCN cross-species atlases
- Herring et al. 2022: PFC gene regulatory dynamics gestation to adulthood

## 7. Glossary
- **Temporal lobe**: Brain region involved in auditory processing, memory, language
- **LTK**: Leukocyte tyrosine kinase — marker of a specific excitatory neuron subtype
- **snRNA-seq**: Single-nucleus RNA-seq
