## One-line Summary
Paired snATAC + snRNA-seq of 38 human neocortical samples (first trimester to adolescence, PFC + V1) with spatial transcriptomics; discovers Tri-IPCs (tripotential intermediate progenitors generating GABAergic neurons, OPCs, and astrocytes); ASD risk enriched in second-trimester intratelencephalic neurons.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 2025

## 2. Key Contributions
- Paired snATAC + snRNA-seq atlas of 38 human neocortical samples spanning first trimester to adolescence
- Prefrontal cortex + primary visual cortex comparison
- Spatial transcriptomics for intercellular communication analysis
- Discovery of Tri-IPCs: tripotential intermediate progenitor cells generating GABAergic neurons, OPCs, and astrocytes
- Lineage tracing reveals complex progenitor relationships during neurogenesis-to-gliogenesis transition
- ASD risk: enriched in second-trimester intratelencephalic neurons
- Glioblastoma cells transcriptomically resemble Tri-IPCs

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- 38 human neocortical samples: first trimester to adolescence; PFC + V1
- Paired snATAC-seq + snRNA-seq (multiome)
- Spatial transcriptomics for cell-cell communication
- Progenitor purification + lineage-tracing experiments

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- Tri-IPCs: tripotential; generate local GABAergic neurons, OPCs, and astrocytes
- Neurogenesis-to-gliogenesis transition involves Tri-IPC intermediate state
- ASD GWAS risk: enriched in second-trimester intratelencephalic neurons
- Glioblastoma transcriptomic resemblance to Tri-IPCs (developmental hijacking hypothesis)
- Cell-type-specific, age-specific, area-specific GRNs characterized

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- 38 samples are relatively few for this developmental span; some stages underrepresented
- Spatial transcriptomics resolution and gene coverage limits intercellular analysis
- Glioblastoma-Tri-IPC resemblance is correlational; functional validation needed

## 6. Related Work
- Herring et al. 2022: human PFC from gestation to adulthood (overlapping timeline)
- Zhang et al. 2025-PFC: human-macaque PFC comparison (complementary)
- Braun et al. 2023: first-trimester atlas (earlier stages)

## 7. Glossary
- **Tri-IPC**: Tripotential intermediate progenitor cell — generates GABAergic neurons, OPCs, and astrocytes
- **Intratelencephalic (IT) neurons**: Cortical excitatory neurons projecting within telencephalon
- **Neurogenesis-to-gliogenesis transition**: Developmental switch when progenitors shift from making neurons to glia
