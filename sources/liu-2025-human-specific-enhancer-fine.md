## One-line Summary
HARE5, a human accelerated region (HAR) enhancer of Frizzled8/WNT signaling, drives human-specific cortical expansion; knock-in of human HARE5 in mice enlarges neocortex via radial glia self-renewal, while human-specific HARE5 variants increase progenitor proliferation in human/chimp organoids.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 31 July 2025 (Vol. 643)

## 2. Key Contributions
- Demonstrated HARE5 (HAR enhancer of Frizzled8) controls cortical development and connectivity via WNT signaling
- Hs-HARE5 knock-in mice: significantly enlarged neocortex with more excitatory neurons; increased functional cortical independence
- Mechanistically: Hs-HARE5 increases radial glia self-renewal early, then expands neurogenic potential
- Four human-specific HARE5 variants drive increased enhancer activity → progenitor proliferation in human/chimp NPCs and organoids
- WNT signaling amplification as mechanism for human cortical expansion

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Genome-edited mouse knock-in of human HARE5
- Fixed and live imaging; lineage tracing; scRNA-seq
- Genome-edited human and chimpanzee NPCs + cortical organoids
- In vivo neural dynamics measurement (cortical independence)
- Enhancer activity reporter assays

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- Hs-HARE5 knock-in mice: neocortex significantly enlarged; more excitatory neurons
- Increased functional independence between cortical regions (in vivo)
- Radial glia: increased self-renewal (early) → expanded neurogenic potential (late)
- Four human-specific HARE5 variants → increased enhancer activity
- WNT signaling amplification is the mechanism

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Mouse model cannot fully recapitulate human cortical complexity
- HARE5 is one of thousands of HARs; combinatorial effects unknown
- Functional consequences of increased cortical independence not fully characterized

## 6. Related Work
- Pollard et al. 2006: HAR discovery

## 7. Glossary
- **HAR**: Human accelerated region — genomic sequence highly conserved across mammals but with human-specific substitutions
- **HARE5**: HAR enhancer of Frizzled8 (WNT receptor)
- **Radial glia**: Cortical progenitor cells; their self-renewal determines neuron number
- **oRG**: Outer radial glia — primate-enriched progenitors for cortical expansion
