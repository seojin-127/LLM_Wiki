## One-line Summary
Three coupled microfluidic organ-on-chip devices (two BBB chips + one brain chip) model the human neurovascular unit (NVU); reveals previously unknown metabolic coupling between BBB endothelium and neurons; models methamphetamine pharmacokinetics across the NVU.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature Biotechnology
- Published: September 2018

## 2. Key Contributions
- First linked multi-chip NVU system: two BBB chips + brain chip in series (influx BBB → brain → efflux BBB)
- Primary human brain microvascular endothelial cells + pericytes + astrocytes (BBB chip) + neurons (brain chip)
- Dissects contributions of individual cell types to NVU function
- Reveals metabolic coupling between BBB endothelium and neurons (previously unknown)
- Models intravascular methamphetamine administration: pharmacokinetics and CNS effects

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Microfluidic organ chips: membrane separating endothelial (vascular) and parenchymal compartments
- BBB chip: hBMVECs (monolayer) + pericytes/astrocytes on membrane
- Brain chip: mixed human neural cells (hippocampus-derived stem cells → ~60% glia, 40% neurons; glutamatergic, GABAergic, dopaminergic, serotonergic)
- Three chips in series with continuous medium flow (artificial blood → artificial CSF)

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- BBB chip shows tight junction formation and selective permeability
- Metabolic coupling: BBB-derived metabolites influence neuronal activity
- Methamphetamine crosses BBB and alters neuronal metabolism in downstream chip
- System resolves cell-type-specific contributions to NVU homeostasis

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Primary cells; iPSC-derived NVU not yet implemented
- Simplified geometry vs. in vivo complexity (single flow path)
- Metabolite identification limited by mass spec sensitivity

## 6. Related Work
- BBB organ-chip prior work (Wyss Institute)
- NVU modeling with conventional 2D culture methods

## 7. Glossary
- **NVU**: Neurovascular unit — functional unit of brain vasculature (endothelium + pericytes + astrocytes + neurons)
- **BBB**: Blood-brain barrier — selectively permeable endothelial barrier
- **hBMVEC**: Human brain microvascular endothelial cells
- **TPS**: Thin-plate splines (for chip geometry, not related to ELD)
- **CSF**: Cerebrospinal fluid
