## One-line Summary
Long-term culture of human cortical spheroids (hCS) beyond 20 months demonstrates progressive maturation matching key early postnatal developmental transitions, including astrogenesis and functional maturation, revealing intrinsic programs of human brain development.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature Neuroscience
- Published: 2021

## 2. Key Contributions
- Demonstrated that human cortical spheroids (hCS) cultured >20 months undergo maturation equivalent to early postnatal human brain development
- Identified intrinsic transcriptional programs driving maturation beyond mid-fetal stages (neurogenesis → astrogenesis → postnatal transitions)
- Showed progressive functional maturation including network activity development
- Provided systematic, unbiased characterization of hCS maturation timeline vs in vivo postnatal development

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Human cortical spheroid (hCS) differentiation from pluripotent stem cells (directed, matrix-free method)
- Long-term culture up to 20+ months
- scRNA-seq time series profiling; comparison to in vivo developmental transcriptomics
- Functional analyses of neuronal network activity
- Benchmarking against human fetal and postnatal brain gene expression datasets

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- hCS progress from neurogenesis → astrogenesis with intrinsic timing matching in vivo postnatal transitions
- Transcriptomic profiles of long-term hCS align with early postnatal human brain stages
- Functional maturation observed at late time points (network activity)
- Intrinsic programs (not extrinsic signals) drive timing of maturation

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Absence of vasculature, microglia, and extrinsic cues limits full recapitulation
- iPSC line variability affects reproducibility
- Full postnatal maturation (years) not achievable in current culture conditions

## 6. Related Work
- Pasca et al. 2015: original hCS method
- Lancaster et al. 2013: cerebral organoids
- Human fetal brain atlas (Nowakowski et al.)

## 7. Glossary
- **hCS**: Human cortical spheroid — directed, matrix-free 3D cortical organoid from pluripotent stem cells
- **Astrogenesis**: Generation of astrocytes; follows neurogenesis in cortical development
- **Postnatal transition**: Switch from prenatal to postnatal gene expression programs at birth
