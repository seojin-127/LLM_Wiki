## One-line Summary
Comprehensive atlas of mouse telencephalic GABAergic neurons (611K adult + 614K developmental cells) establishes a 7-class, 52-subclass, 1,051-cluster taxonomy, reveals widespread long-distance migration, and shows that cortical and striatal types diversify postnatally.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 2025

## 2. Key Contributions
- Adult taxonomy: 7 classes, 52 subclasses, 284 supertypes, 1,051 clusters from 611K cells
- Developmental taxonomy: 1,688 clusters from 614K developmental cells
- Long-distance migration is a common feature across many GABAergic neuron types, not an exception
- Cortical and striatal GABAergic neuron subtypes diversify primarily postnatally (not embryonically)
- Spatial organization mapped at subtype resolution across telencephalon

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- snRNA-seq of adult mouse telencephalon (611K cells) and developmental time series (614K cells)
- Spatial transcriptomics for anatomical localization of subtypes
- Hierarchical taxonomy construction (class → subclass → supertype → cluster)
- Developmental trajectory analysis from progenitor to mature neuron stages

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- Adult: 7 classes encompass all telencephalic GABAergic diversity
- 52 subclasses, 284 supertypes, 1,051 terminal clusters
- Developmental dataset: 1,688 clusters revealing progressive diversification
- Long-distance migration: many subtypes originate far from final anatomical position
- Cortical interneuron subtypes: transcriptomic diversification peaks postnatally (P7–P21)
- Striatal projection neuron subtypes: similar postnatal maturation window

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Mouse only; human GABAergic taxonomy pending
- Spatial resolution limits fine mapping within small nuclei
- Developmental series not single-cell tracked (pseudotime-based inference)

## 6. Related Work
- Yao et al. 2023: whole mouse brain atlas (broader scope)
- Langlieb et al. 2023: molecular cytoarchitecture of adult mouse brain
- Corrigan et al. 2025: cross-species striatal interneuron conservation

## 7. Glossary
- **GABAergic neuron**: Inhibitory neuron using GABA as neurotransmitter
- **Telencephalon**: Anterior brain region comprising cortex, striatum, hippocampus, and amygdala
- **Taxonomy levels**: Class > Subclass > Supertype > Cluster (hierarchical from coarse to fine)
- **Long-distance migration**: Interneurons born in ganglionic eminences migrate tangentially to cortex, striatum, and other targets
