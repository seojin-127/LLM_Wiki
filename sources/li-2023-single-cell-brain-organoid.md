## One-line Summary
CHOOSE (CRISPR–Human Organoids–Single-cell Sequencing) system enables pooled loss-of-function screening of 36 high-risk ASD genes in cerebral organoids, revealing that dorsal intermediate progenitors, ventral progenitors, and upper-layer excitatory neurons are most vulnerable; ARID1B mutation specifically disrupts oligodendrocyte and interneuron precursor fate transitions.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Nature
- Published: 14 September 2023

## 2. Key Contributions
- CHOOSE system: pooled CRISPR-Cas9 loss-of-function screening in mosaic cerebral organoids with scRNA-seq readout
- Screened 36 high-risk ASD transcriptional regulator genes in a single pooled experiment
- Most vulnerable cell types: dorsal intermediate progenitors, ventral progenitors, upper-layer excitatory neurons
- ARID1B (BAF complex subunit): disrupts fate transition of progenitors to oligodendrocyte + interneuron precursors
- Constructed developmental GRN from scRNA-seq + chromatin data; ASD-associated regulatory modules identified

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- Verified guide RNA pairs for reliable KO in organoids
- Inducible CRISPR-Cas9 in mosaic organoids (not all cells perturbed)
- Pooled screening: multiple KOs in same organoid batch; guide RNA recovered from scRNA-seq
- scRNA-seq + chromatin accessibility for GRN construction
- Validation in patient-specific iPSC-derived organoids (ARID1B patients)

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- 36 ASD risk genes screened simultaneously in cerebral organoids
- Dorsal IPs, ventral progenitors, upper-layer excitatory neurons: most affected cell types
- BAF complex perturbations → enrichment of ventral telencephalon progenitors
- ARID1B KO → impaired fate transition to OPC + interneuron precursors (confirmed in patient iPSCs)
- ASD-associated + perturbation-enriched regulatory modules identified in GRN

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Cerebral organoid model: incomplete recapitulation of in vivo development
- Mosaic KO may dilute phenotypic effects; incomplete penetrance
- GRN is correlative; functional validation of individual nodes needed

## 6. Related Work
- Martinscosta et al. 2024: ARID1B callosal organoid model (same gene, different phenotype)
- Jin et al. 2020: In vivo Perturb-Seq of ASD risk genes (complementary in vivo approach)
- He et al. 2024 (HNOCA): atlas for mapping perturbed organoid states

## 7. Glossary
- **CHOOSE**: CRISPR-Human Organoids-Single-cell Sequencing — pooled organoid perturbation screening platform
- **Mosaic organoid**: Organoid where only subset of cells carry the perturbation
- **BAF complex**: BRG1/BRM-associated factor — chromatin remodeling complex; multiple subunits are ASD risk genes
- **OPC**: Oligodendrocyte precursor cell
