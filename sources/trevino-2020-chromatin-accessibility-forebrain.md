## One-line Summary
ATAC-seq + RNA-seq profiling over 20 months of 3D dorsal/ventral forebrain organoids reveals chromatin accessibility dynamics matching in vivo human brain; identifies putative enhancers active in specific neuronal lineages and maps neurodevelopmental disease risk variants onto cell-type/time-point-specific chromatin.

## 1. Document Info
- Journal/Conference: Science
- Published: 2020

## 2. Key Contributions
- Generated ATAC-seq + RNA-seq time course (20 months) of dorsal and ventral forebrain organoids from human pluripotent stem cells
- Showed organoid chromatin accessibility recapitulates in vivo patterns
- Identified putative enhancers active in specific neuronal/glial lineages
- Mapped disease risk variants (ASD, schizophrenia, epilepsy) onto cell-type and time-point-specific open chromatin regions
- Identified cell types and developmental periods of increased disease susceptibility

## 3. Methods & Architecture
- 3D directed forebrain organoid differentiation: dorsal (cortical) and ventral (ganglionic eminence)
- ATAC-seq + RNA-seq at multiple time points over 20 months
- Direct comparison with primary human brain tissue chromatin data
- Disease risk variant enrichment analysis in cell-type-specific open chromatin

## 4. Key Results & Benchmarks
- Organoid chromatin accessibility matches in vivo human brain tissue across time
- Dynamic enhancer landscape resolved: progenitor → neuron → glia transitions
- Disease risk variants (ASD, SCZ, epilepsy) enriched in cell-type-specific open chromatin
- Specific neuronal lineages and developmental windows identified as disease-relevant

## 5. Limitations & Future Work
- Organoid model limitations (no vasculature, microglia)
- ATAC-seq on bulk organoid tissue (not single-cell in original); limited cell-type resolution
- Correlation of chromatin ↔ gene expression not always linear

## 6. Related Work
- Trevino et al. 2021 (Cell): single-cell multi-omic version
- Herring et al. 2022: PFC chromatin dynamics
- ENCODE brain regulatory elements

## 7. Glossary
- **ATAC-seq**: Assay for Transposase-Accessible Chromatin sequencing — maps open chromatin
- **Putative enhancer**: Open chromatin region not overlapping a promoter; inferred to have enhancer function
- **Forebrain organoid**: 3D model of dorsal (cortex) or ventral (GE) forebrain from PSCs
